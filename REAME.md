PROJECT OVERVIEW
Description
AnHu Food is a comprehensive mobile food delivery application developed for Android and iOS platforms. The app connects customers, restaurants, and shippers within a modern delivery ecosystem with features such as food ordering, online payment, real-time order tracking, AI & admin chat support.
Objectives
Provide a convenient and fast online food ordering experience


Integrate secure online payment (VNPay)


Real-time order tracking with SignalR


24/7 customer support with AI Chatbot (Google Gemini)


Efficient order management for admins and shippers
